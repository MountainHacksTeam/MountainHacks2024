ABOUT

A 14-hour hackathon for high-school students taking place on March 16, 2023 at Mountain House High School!

WHO?

Open to all high-school students grades 9-12, and to all experience levels! Whether you're a beginner or advanced, you're invited to MountainHacks 2024.

WHAT?

An in-person hackathon with tons of cool stuff: food, swag (merch), workshops, prizes, and dev tools for ALL hackers!

WHY?

Whether you're looking to learn new coding skills or build revolutionary apps and win prizes, MountainHacks 2024 is for YOU! We'll have developer tools, opportunities, and workshops for everyone.
